Discover Pokemon Play

Pokemon play has been created as a user friendly based ‘play to earn’ game. This allows users to earn PPC (Pokemon play coin) PPC. User’s will have the opportunity to download pokemon play to android, iOS and also desktop. The contents of the game will include different levels which the player must complete in order to earn their tokens. To complete each level and earn PPC tokens the player’s will have their own character running through different levels to collect the ‘energy balls’, the player will be able to see these throughout the game, the amount of energy balls collected will reflect the amount of PPC the player earns!  

Once the player completes level 1 they will unlock level 2! On level 2 the player will be able to use their PPC tokens on accessories for their characters, this will include NFT jackets and shoes for the characters in the game, the pokemon play NFT jacket will have a magnetic field which will attract any energy balls that are nearby allowing players to collect double the amount as they run past! The NFT shoes will allow characters to run faster on the next levels allowing the player to collect energy balls at a faster pace than the previous levels. Players will also have the option to collect the Pokemon play ‘secret boxes’ containing a special secret surprise!  

Players do not have to spend all their tokens while playing the game they will also have the option to swap their PPC tokens for cash via the DEX exchange platform where PPC tokens are listed, Players can also find PPC listed on other exchanges such as Pancake swap, Biswap and Apeswap.
